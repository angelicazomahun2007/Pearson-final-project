    Breathing Exercise

    Use this 4-7-8 technique to calm your nervous system:

    Breathe In

    Inhale deeply for 4 seconds

    How it works:

    1. Breathe in quietly through your nose for 4 seconds
    2. Hold your breath for 7 seconds
    3. Exhale completely through your mouth for 8 seconds
    4. Repeat 3-4 times

    Urge Surfing Technique

    Ride out your craving like a wave until it passes naturally.

    Notice the craving without acting on it

    Key Steps:

    1. Acknowledge the craving without judgment
    2. Imagine it as a wave that builds, peaks, and subsides
    3. Focus on your breathing as you "ride" the wave
    4. Notice how the craving changes over time

    Positive Affirmations

    Repeat these affirmations to strengthen your recovery mindset.

    I am stronger than my cravings.

    Try This:

    • Say the affirmation out loud 3 times
    • Write it down in a visible place
    • Repeat it whenever cravings arise
