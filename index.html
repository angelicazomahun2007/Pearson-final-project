    Understanding the Many Faces of Addiction

    Addiction comes in many forms‚Äîknowing them is the first step toward awareness and healing.

    Substance Addiction

    Substance addiction involves the harmful use of drugs or alcohol, leading to physical and mental dependence

    Read more

    Behavioral Addiction

    A pattern of compulsive behaviors like gambling, gaming, or social media use, driven by emotional or mental urges rather than substances.

    Read more

    Emotional / Relationship Addiction

    An unhealthy dependence on relationships, affection, or emotional highs that can lead to toxic patterns, codependency, and loss of self-identity.
